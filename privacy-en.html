Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 04, 2026

At SignalEdge (signaledgeapp.com) we take user privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it and what your rights are.

1. Data controller

The data controller is the owner of SignalEdge, reachable at signaledge.app@gmail.com. SignalEdge operates from Spain and is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name and email address provided when signing in with Google.
  • Usage data: app preferences (leverage, timeframe, capital), trade history entered by the user, pinned signals and price alerts.
  • Analytics data: anonymous browsing data collected by Google Analytics 4 (pages visited, duration, device). No personally identifiable information is collected through GA4.

3. Purpose and legal basis

  • Service provision (legal basis: contract performance) — to sync your data across devices via Supabase.
  • App improvement (legal basis: legitimate interest) — via anonymous Google Analytics data.
  • Communications — we do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

4. Third-party providers

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — cloud data storage. Servers in the EU. Privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous web analytics. You can disable it with extensions like uBlock Origin. Google policy.
  • Binance API — public BTC/USDT price data. No user data is transmitted.
  • Google OAuth — authentication. We only receive public name, email and profile photo.

5. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data will be deleted within 30 days.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: correct incorrect data.
  • Erasure: delete your account and all your data.
  • Portability: export your data (available from the app in CSV format).
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data for analytical purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, write to us at signaledge.app@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We only use cookies necessary for the app to function (user session) and Google Analytics 4 cookies for statistical purposes. We do not use advertising or third-party cookies for commercial purposes.

You can manage cookies from your browser settings or reject analytics from the cookie banner when you first visit.

8. Security

All data is transmitted encrypted via HTTPS. Database access is protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring each user can only access their own data.

9. Minors

SignalEdge is not directed at anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes via a notice in the app. The last updated date will always be visible at the top of this document.

Trading cryptocurrencies involves a high risk of capital loss. Never invest more than you can afford to lose. SignalEdge is not responsible for investment decisions made based on information shown in the app.
